About Us

A small team of senior engineers helping companies build better infrastructure since 2019.

Our Story

Skybridge Systems was founded in Amsterdam in 2019 by a group of infrastructure engineers who had spent the previous decade building and operating large-scale platforms at companies like Booking.com, Adyen, and TomTom.

We noticed the same pattern everywhere: talented product teams held back by fragile infrastructure, manual deployments, and mounting cloud bills. We started Skybridge to give growing companies access to the same calibre of infrastructure expertise that only the largest enterprises could traditionally afford.

Today our team of eight consultants works with clients across the Netherlands, Germany, and the Nordics — always hands-on, always senior.

How We Work

1. Assess

We start with a thorough review of your current architecture, pipelines, and operational processes. You receive a detailed report with prioritised recommendations.

2. Implement

Our engineers embed with your team to execute the roadmap — writing Terraform, setting up Kubernetes, building CI pipelines, or migrating workloads.

3. Transfer

We document everything, run knowledge-transfer sessions, and stay available for questions. The goal is always to make ourselves unnecessary.

The Team

Our consultants have an average of 12 years of industry experience spanning systems engineering, security, and software development. We keep the team deliberately small so every client gets direct access to senior talent — no layers of account managers in between.

We are based in Amsterdam but work remotely with clients throughout Europe. On-site visits are arranged when it makes sense for workshops, onboarding, or incident response.
